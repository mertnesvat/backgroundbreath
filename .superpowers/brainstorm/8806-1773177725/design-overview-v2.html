FloatingPanel

NSPanel subclass. .floatingWindowLevel, non-activating, click-through, transparent, draggable by background.

BreathTimer

ObservableObject. @Published phase: .inhale / .exhale. Timer fires every 5.5s, toggles phase. Pause/resume.
